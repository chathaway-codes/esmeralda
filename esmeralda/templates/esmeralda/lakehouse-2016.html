Directions from Fishing Access Rd.

Althought it looks like Google Maps now takes you to the correct place, here are some directions just in case (alt. look for signs marked "Hathaway"):

  1. After going onto the dirt road, continue past the first intersection to the fork in the road just past the steep hill
  2. Take the right path
  3. Stay on the road; you should pass "Elbow Dr" on your right. When you see the boat trailer parked before a small shed, park in front the boat trailer
  4. Go to the right, down the stairs, and you will arrive!

What to bring

The lakehouse has electricity, wifi, and even air conditioning. We should have plenty of linens and pillows; although some people may need to sleep on air mattresses or pull out beds. Bring anything personal you need, such as:

  • Toothbrush and toothpaste
  • Bathing suite and towel
  • Clothing; we have a washer and dryer
  • Favorite Board Games
  • Snacks, alcohol, etc.

Food will be bought by myself, but alas, I am a mere penniless graduate student. Any donations towards the food and boat gas for the weekend would be appreciated.
